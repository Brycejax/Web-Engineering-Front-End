Our Services

The Lfoods services provides college students or organizations to place orders for foods and catering.



Our Misson

Our misson is to provide a simple UI design for students to order food around their busy schedule. Since the Student Center options close around 7 o' clock in the evening, LFoods exist for convience of food for our students here at Lipscomb.



Our Audience

Our audience consist of mostly Lipscomb students and Faculty. LFoods does not limit the users to only this however, it is accessible to anybody near or around Nashville.
